Brief Opinions

Supreme Court Opinions, Only Briefer

'Brief Opinions' takes the original language of Supreme Court opinions and decides what to carefully edit from them in order to deliver you the briefest version possible that still contains the most important information. It is the most efficent way to stay "briefed" on any Supreme Court decision, new or old.
